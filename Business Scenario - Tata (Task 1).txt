Questions for the CEO (Business Growth & Revenue Focus)
1. What are our top-performing products in terms of sales and revenue?
2. Which customer segments contribute the most to our overall revenue?
3. Which geographic regions or sales channels (e.g., website, mobile app) are driving the highest revenue?
4. How has our revenue and profit changed over the past year, and what trends should we focus on?

Questions for the CMO (Marketing & Customer Engagement Focus)
1. Which marketing campaigns have been the most successful in driving sales and customer engagement?
2. Which customer demographics (age, location, purchase behavior) respond best to our marketing efforts?
3. What is our customer retention rate, and how can we improve repeat purchases?
4. Which advertising platforms (social media, email, search ads) generate the highest return on investment (ROI)?
